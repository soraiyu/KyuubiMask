KyuubiMask completely hides your notification content—no one can see what you received, even if they're looking right at your screen. Notification content is never displayed or saved by this app.

KyuubiMask is a privacy-first notification masking tool that protects your sensitive information during screen sharing, presentations, or when your screen is visible to others.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• No internet permission - works completely offline
• No data collection or storage
• No tracking, analytics, or ads
• Notification content is never logged or saved
• Open source with Apache 2.0 license

✨ FEATURES
• Masks notifications from any app you choose with generic text
• Tapping a masked notification opens the original app directly, without revealing any content
• Built-in presets for Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, and LINE
• Per-app toggle to choose which apps to mask
• Quick Settings tile for one-tap masking toggle
• Simple on/off toggle to enable or disable the service
• Works in the background
• Minimal permissions required
• Lightweight and efficient

📱 SUPPORTED APPS
KyuubiMask works with any installed app — select additional targets via the "Add custom apps…" button in Settings. Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, and LINE are available as built-in presets.

🎯 PERFECT FOR
• Screen sharing during video calls
• Live presentations and demos
• Recording tutorials or videos
• Working in public spaces
• Protecting your privacy

💡 HOW IT WORKS
When enabled, KyuubiMask replaces notification content from supported apps with generic text like "New notification", hiding sensitive information while still allowing you to see that you received a notification.

📖 WANT TO CHECK A NOTIFICATION LATER?
This app never saves notification content. If you want to review what you received, use Android's built-in Notification History (Settings → Notifications → Notification History).

🦊 OPEN SOURCE
All code is available on GitHub. No hidden functionality, complete transparency.
